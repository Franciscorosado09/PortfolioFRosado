About Me

My name is Francisco Eduardo Rosado, but most people know me as Frankie. I have spent the last 7 years working in public service, first with the Peace Corps in Burkina Faso and most recently at the United States Digital Service (USDS) in Washington, DC. At USDS I supported the top technologist in the nation in their mission to change public facing services to help millions of Americans. It was at USDS that I began to understand the power of programming which inspired me to make a career change to become a Full-Stack developer.
